Rules of Play

  1. Begin play by clicking on any of the blank squares on the Match Game Play Board to reveal a picture.
  2. Click on a second blank square to reveal a second picture.
  3. If the pictures match, they will disappear and you may continue per Step. 1 to find another matching pair.
  4. If the pictures do not match, they will return to their original state.
  5. When you have matched all pairs of pictures, you win!
  6. Enjoy the game!
